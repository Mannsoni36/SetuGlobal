## Matching you to the right German university

Germany has hundreds of public and private universities, and rankings alone don't tell you which one is right for you. We weigh admission requirements, tuition (public universities are €0–€350/semester in administrative fees, private ones run €5,000–€20,000/year), city living costs, and how well a program lines up with your career goals.

We maintain direct relationships with admissions offices across Germany, so we know current intake numbers, deadlines, and what each university actually wants to see in an Indian applicant's file — not just what's published on their website.
