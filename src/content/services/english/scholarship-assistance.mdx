## Making Germany even more affordable

Public universities in Germany already charge little to no tuition, but scholarships and grants can cover living costs, travel, and more — if you apply for the right ones, the right way. We screen you against DAAD scholarships, university-specific grants, and merit-based awards you actually qualify for.

Rather than a generic list, we help you build the application case for each one: which achievements to highlight, how to frame your motivation letter, and when to apply relative to your university deadlines.
