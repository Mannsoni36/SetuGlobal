## Somewhere to live before you land

Finding student housing in Germany from India is one of the more stressful parts of the move — dormitory waitlists open early, and private listings move fast. We start your housing search well before your departure date so you're not arranging accommodation from an airport.

We weigh university dormitories against private apartments and shared flats (WG), factoring in your budget and how far you're willing to commute to campus.
