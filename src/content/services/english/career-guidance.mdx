## Planning past graduation, not just admission

Getting admitted is only half the journey — what happens after graduation matters just as much. Germany offers an 18-month Job Seeker Visa for graduates, and international students can legally work up to 120 full days a year during their studies, which makes early career planning worthwhile.

We help you build a German-market-ready resume (Lebenslauf format expectations differ from Indian resumes), prepare for interviews, and put together a realistic job search strategy for your field.
